About me

Hey there! I’m Andy, here is a short spot on me, for those of you interested. Photography isn’t just a hobby for me, it’s a daily ritual that fuels my creativity and keeps me connected to the world around me. Since moving to Nosara in 2015, I’ve been fortunate enough to call this stretch of Costa Rican coast my home.

When I’m not behind the camera, you’ll find me surfing or spending time with my family. Family is at the heart of everything I do. As a proud dad to three wonderful kids, I’ve learned to see the world through fresh eyes, appreciating the small, beautiful moments that often go unnoticed. That perspective shapes my photography, grounded in genuine connection.

From the epic beaches of Playa Grande to the lonley roads of Camaronal, I roam the coastline always hunting the perfect shot. My goal is simple, to give you images that feel personal, timeless, and true to the spirit of the ocean you love.

I’d be honored to create something special for you. Let’s capture those unforgettable moments worth saving.

Cheers!
Andy
